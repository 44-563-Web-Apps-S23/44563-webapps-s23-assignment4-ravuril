Lakshmi Karthik Ravuri "snips a play"

THE TWELVE-POUND LOOK    _Sir James M. Barrie_
SIR HARRY.

[_Rather anxious for corroboration._]

Lord, how she winced when I said I was to give you those ropes of pearls.

LADY SIMS.

Did she? I didn't notice. I suppose so.

SIR HARRY.

[_Frowning._]

Suppose? Surely I know enough about women to know that.
